Octave Mariotti

I am a post-doctoral researcher in Computer Vision and Machine Leraning at the School of Informatics of the University of Edinburgh. I am part of the Visual Computing Group and the Visual AI project , working principally with Hakan Bilen and Oisin Mac Aodha .

My research interests cover learning without human annotations, 3D awareness in deep image models and robustness in evaluation.

I completed my PhD in 2022 at the University of Edinburgh under the supervision of Hakan Bilen. Before that, I obtained a Bachelor and Master degrees in theoretical computer science from the ENS de Lyon .
